Life Cycle



  • Egg(3-5 Days) - The eggs are laid in the clusters and covered with white hairs


  • Larva(14-28 Days) - Inverted "Y" marking in head area.Four smaller dorsal spots in a trapeze arrangement on most other segment.Four large dorsal spots in a near square arrangement on second last segment.


  • Pupa(7-14 Days) - After development ,they crawl to the ground to pupate in the soil


  • Adult(11-14 Days) -Adult moths live for 11-14 days. They are blown in by storms, migrating thousands of miles
